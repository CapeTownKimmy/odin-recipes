Mini Pizza Mummies

These Mini Pizza Mummies will be a hit with the kids! Simple and quick to prepare, this dish is ideal for their lunchbox or on-the-go meal. For an added treat, experiment with different topping combinations!

Ingredients:

  • 320g ready rolled puff pastry
  • 100g passata
  • 1 garlic clove, crushed
  • 2 tbsp sun-dried tomato paste
  • 150g cherry tomatoes sliced
  • 75g mozzarella cheese, cut into strips
  • Olives, cheddar, cheese and veggies for decoration

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C.
  2. Unroll the puff pastry and cut out 4x10cm circles. Place them in a baking sheet lined with baking paper. Prick the base with a fork.
  3. Mix the passata, garlic and sun-dried tomato in a bowl. Spread over the bases, top with tomatoes, basil and cheese.
  4. Cook the pizzas for 15-20 minutes until the cheese is bubbling and golden. Decorate with your favourite toppings, using the olives for eyes. Allow to cool before serving.
