Very Berry Breakfast Smoothie

Description:

Our Very Berry Breakfast Smoothie has something for everyone; even picky eaters will enjoy it! Our smoothie is jam-packed with nutrients that will help your kids make the most of their day! Super easy and quick to prepare even on those rushed school mornings.

Ingredients:

  • 1 small ripe banana
  • Approx 140g blackberries, blueberries, raspberries or strawberries (or use a mix), plus extra to serve
  • 1/2 cup of plain yoghurt
  • 1 tbsp rolled oats
  • Runny honey, to serve

Steps:

  1. Slice the banana into your blender or food processor and add the berries of your choice.
  2. Whizz until smooth before adding the plain yoghurt and rolled oats.
  3. Keep blending until the mixture is smooth enough to drink.
  4. Pour the mix into a glass, toss a few extra fruits on top, drizzle with honey and serve.
